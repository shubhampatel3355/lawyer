SAFE Agreement (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)

A SAFE Agreement lets you accept money from investors today in exchange for equity at a future date—typically during your next priced round. It’s fast, founder-friendly, and avoids complex share structuring when your startup is still evolving.

  1. When raising early-stage capital
  2. When valuation is unclear
  3. During bridge funding rounds
  4. When simplifying fundraising
  1. Zero valuation pressure
  2. Clean documentation
  3. Investor trust, founder control
  4. Automatic conversion
Valuation Cap

Sets max company value for conversion

Trigger Events

Auto-convert during raise, sale, or IPO

No Debt Obligation

Not a loan—no interest, no maturity

Dispute Resolution

Arbitration-ready for fast conflict resolution

Deliverables
  1. Legally drafted SAFE Agreement
  2. Covers cap, discount, trigger events, and investor rights
  3. Consultation with startup-focused lawyer
  4. First draft in 24–48 hours
  5. Two free iterations after delivery
